Abel Soares Siqueira on Inspiring Computing Podcast

Abel Soares Siqueira was recently featured on the Inspiring Computing podcast, where he discussed his work on JuliaSmoothOptimizers and his role as a research software engineer at the Netherlands eScience Center.

In this episode, Abel covers:

  • His transition from academia in Brazil to research software engineering in the Netherlands

  • Why he chose Julia for scientific computing and optimization

  • The design and goals of Julia Smooth Optimizers

  • Managing interdependent packages and the importance of automation in testing and documentation

  • His contributions to the Julia community through YouTube and open collaboration

You can listen to the full episode and read the detailed show notes on Buzzsprout: Behind the Code: Abel's Contributions to Julia Smooth Optimizers and Research Software Engineering
